Green Dentistry

Caring for Smiles,
Caring for Nature.

We believe that world-class dental care shouldn't come at a cost to the environment. Our Badulla clinic is designed to be as eco-friendly as it is effective.

View Our Initiatives

Why Go Green?

Traditional dentistry produces a significant amount of plastic waste, chemical runoff, and energy consumption. We have taken a stand to change that.

By adopting "Green Dentistry" principles, we reduce our carbon footprint while providing a safer, cleaner environment for our patients and staff in the Uva Province.

Our Eco-Friendly Initiatives

Small changes make a big impact. Here is how we are making a difference every day.

Paperless Charting

We have transitioned to fully digital patient records and appointment scheduling, saving thousands of sheets of paper annually.

Responsible Waste

We strictly segregate clinical waste and use amalgam separators to prevent mercury and other metals from entering the water supply.

Energy Efficiency

Our clinic utilizes LED lighting throughout and energy-efficient dental equipment to minimize our power consumption.

Our Promise to the Future

Sustainability is not a one-time goal; it is a continuous journey. Specialist Dental Service pledges to:

  • Continuously reduce single-use plastics.
  • Source eco-friendly office supplies.
  • Educate patients on sustainable oral care.
