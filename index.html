Answers to Your Dental Questions

Common questions about our services and procedures.

How often should I visit the dentist?
For most people, we recommend a check-up and cleaning every six months to maintain optimal oral health and catch issues early.
Are your orthodontic treatments painful?
Modern orthodontics are much more comfortable than in the past. You may experience minor discomfort after adjustments, but it is usually brief and manageable.
Do you handle emergency dental cases?
Yes, we try to prioritize dental emergencies. Please call us immediately if you are experiencing severe pain or trauma.
