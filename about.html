About Our Dental Clinic

SDS Badulla – Specialist Dental Service SDS Badulla is a well-established Specialist Dental Service committed to delivering high-quality and reliable dental care. With over 10 years of professional experience, we specialize in a wide range of dental treatments tailored to meet individual patient needs. Our journey began as a dental clinic located at Medilro Hospital, Badulla. Today, SDS Badulla is independently managed, continuing to grow while maintaining the highest standards of care and professionalism. Over the past decade, we have successfully treated more than 500 patients, earning their trust through excellence, safety, and compassionate service. SDS Badulla was founded by Mr. D.F.A.D Perera with a vision to provide exceptional dental services using modern techniques and quality care. We are dedicated to delivering consistent, high-quality service and ensuring patient satisfaction at every visit.

Our Managing Director

Mr. D.F.A.D Perera is a passionate business professional and the Co-Founder of Specialist Dental Clinic, Badulla. He began his career in the plantation sector and brings over 23 years of experience in the industry. With a strong interest in healthcare-sector businesses, he provides strategic leadership and vision. Mr. Perera was educated at S. Thomas’ College, Bandarawela, and holds an MBA and a BSc in Plantation Management from the University of Wayamba. He is a member of AIPM and is recognized as a leading businessman in the Uva Province.
