Smile Solutions for Every Need

Whether it's a check-up or a full makeover, we've got you covered with specialized care in Badulla.

Restorative Dentistry

We restore the function and integrity of your teeth. Our services include bridges, metal & porcelain crowns, and high-quality tooth fillings.

Orthodontics

Straighten your smile with modern solutions. We offer traditional upper & lower braces, as well as clear retainers and invisible aligners.

OMF Surgery

Specialized Oral and Maxillofacial treatments managed with care, including wisdom tooth extractions, frenectomy, and biopsies.

Flexible Dentures

Comfortable, removable replacement options for missing teeth, designed for a natural look and feel.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle care for children of all ages. Fun, friendly visits with preventive care to support healthy growing smiles.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solutions that restore natural function and aesthetics with durable implant options.

Answers to Your Dental Care Questions

Get the clarity you need to feel confident about your oral health decisions.

Do you accept insurance?
We accept most major insurance providers and are happy to assist with claims. Please contact our front desk to verify your specific plan eligibility.
Is teeth whitening safe?
Yes, when performed by a dental professional, teeth whitening is very safe. We use approved methods that protect your enamel while removing stains.
How often should I visit the dentist?
We generally recommend a check-up and cleaning every six months for most patients to maintain optimal oral health.
What if I have dental anxiety?
It's very common! Our team is trained in gentle care and can offer various sedation options to ensure your visit is completely stress-free.
